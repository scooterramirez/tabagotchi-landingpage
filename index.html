What is this?

It’s a chrome extension that helps you escape distracting co-workers in the office. Just press the button in your browser and it’ll immediately send a call to your phone, which you can use to excuse yourself from those pesky colleagues.

Why did you do this?

Everyone knows what it’s like to be working on something really important, only to be distracted by that one co-worker who is always asking “Hey, can I have a minute?” So we made something to fix that problem.

What happens if I answer the call??

You’ll just have to find out for yourself!

Will it work on office phones with extensions?

Unfortunately, not at this time. We opted to stick to 10 digit phone numbers for now.

Who’s behind this?

We’re Breather, a network of beautiful, inspiring workspaces located in cities throughout the US and around the world. We’re all about creating quiet, distraction-free spaces where you can focus on some serious heads-down work without the usual interruptions. The company was founded on the idea that sometimes it helps to ditch the office.
